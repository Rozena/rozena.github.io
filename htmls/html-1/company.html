Core Values

Being part of Cherry Servers is not all about what we do, but also how we do it.We all share the same set of core values here and are proud to do things in the way we believe is right and makes us proud. Feel free to check what people say about us.

Seamless Cooperation “Bring out the best in each other”

We strongly believe that working hand in hand is the finest way of achieving the best results and creating a lasting impact for our clients. We value diversity, celebrate different personalities and respect contrasting opinions, all of which allow us to create enabling environment, where cooperation and team spirit prosper. Whether you are our client or colleague

Heartfelt Communication “Cherish the way you do business”

Every day in every way we embrace a H2H (Human to Human) business model by being genuine, friendly and supportive to each other and our clients. Business is always about making human lives better and delivering outstanding value, however we think that the way of doing so is equally important. This is the reason of why we are implementing a Human Touch Approach in everything we do.

Full Responsibility “Take ownership over your work”

We strongly believe that freedom is essential in order for human potential to flourish. This is why we have chosen to work in a management-free environment and give our employees total control over their work. This not only makes everyone personally responsible for achieving their goals, but also inspires perfection and dedication.

Daily Improvement “Unlock your superpower”

Everyone has a room for improvement. Even more, we believe that every person has a superpower and can unlock his unique potential through continuous growth and learning. We are never satisfied with what we know and can do today. This is why we are always stretching ourselves and striving to become the best versions of ourselves.

Crystal Clarity “Allow no bullsh*t!”

Truth is our way of doing business. We strive to be Crystal Clear in everything we do and tolerate no ambiguity. It is imperative for us to always remain honest and sincere, as we believe that it is the best way to remain trustworthy and build lasting relationships. We allow no bullsh*t in our communication.

Leadership and Dedication “Do your best every day”

We are passionate and enthusiastic in what we do. This is why we work hard every single day trying to catch excellence. We dare to think BIG and act boldly when it comes to taking new initiatives and overcoming our challenges. We do so with thoroughness and precision. All this help us deliver inspiring results to our cherished clients.
